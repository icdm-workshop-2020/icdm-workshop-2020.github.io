Program Chair/Co-Chair
Name: Dr. Fuxiang Chen
Short Bio:
Dr. Fuxiang Chen is currently a Senior Research Scientist in DeepSearch Inc., Korea/Singapore. Prior to that, he was a Research Scientist in Clova AI Research, Naver Corp., Korea. During his career stint, he has held different capacities such as in research, management, teaching and technical roles. He received his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, and a Master of Technology from the National University of Singapore.  Dr. Chen has published his work in top-tier conferences such as EMNLP, AAAI, FSE, etc. He has also served as the Special Track Chair, Session Chair/Co-Chair, Web Chair and Programme Committee member in different conferences. His current research interests lie within the cross-cutting areas in ML, NLP and Data Mining, especially in the Financial domain. 		 
Program Chair/Co-Chair
Name: Dr. Zia Hasan
Short Bio:
Dr. Zia Hasan is currently working as an Applied Research Scientist at Amazon in Seattle, USA where he is currently working on products to improve search experience for Amazon customers. Prior to that, he worked as Senior Research Scientist at Clova AI Research, Naver Corporation and as Senior Researcher at Samsung Electronics, Korea. He received his PhD and MASc degrees from University of British Columbia, Vancouver, Canada and undergraduate degree from the Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, India. He has published extensively in top-tier conferences and journals and served as chair and technical program committee member for several conferences. His current research interests are in the areas of natural language processing, unsupervised learning and generative modeling.
