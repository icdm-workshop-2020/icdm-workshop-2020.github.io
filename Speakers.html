Invited Speakers

Dr. Yuan Luo
Tookitaki Holding Pte. Ltd.

Dr. Yuan Luo is currently working as a Staff Researcher in Tookitaki, Singapore, where his primary focus is applying various machine learning techniques to regulatory products. Before joining Tookitaki, he was working as a postdoc in theoretical physics. He received his PhD degree from National University of Singapore and an undergraduate degree from Sichuan University, China, both in physics. His current research interests include unsupervised learning, machine learning on graphs and their applications to RegTech use cases.
